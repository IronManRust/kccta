Information

Located in the far west Chicago suburb of Batavia, the Kirkland Chase Courtyard Townhomes offer a blend of Fox Valley charm and Chicago sensibility.

This website is intended for the members of the Kirkland Chase Courtyard Townhome Association.

KCCTA employs Northwest Property Management as their association property manager. Details about dues, maintenance requests and other information is available on their website.
